## Formule Chiave

### 1. Gluten Development
```
G' = G'_min + (G'_max - G'_min) × (1 - exp(-k_mix × t_mix))
```

### 2. Fermentation (CO2 production)
```
V_CO2 = V_0 × exp(μ × t)
```
Dove μ = specific growth rate

### 3. Oven Spring
```
H_final = H_0 × (1 + ε × T_oven / T_0)
```
Tipico ε = 1.5-2.5

## Parametri Pizza

### Flour Requirements

| Parametro | Value | Note |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Protein | 10-12% | Medium strength |
| Ash | 0.50-0.65% | Type "00" |
| W value | 180-250 | Pasting properties |
| P/L ratio | 0.5-0.8 | Tenacity/extension |

### Hydration
```
Hydration = 55-65% (baker's %)
```
- Higher = more bubbly crust
- Lower = more compact crust

### Fermentation
| Tempo | Temperatura | Effetto |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 2-4h | 20-24°C | Bulk fermentation |
| 30min | 20-24°C | Ball shaping |

## Chemistry during Baking

### 1. Maillard Reactions
- Starts at >150°C
- Creates crust color and flavor
- Optimal: 200-230°C for 6-10 min

### 2. Starch Gelatinization
- T_gel ≈ 60-70°C for wheat
- Creates structure and texture
- Complete by ~90°C core

### 3. Cheese Melting
- T_melt ≈ 90-120°C for mozzarella
- Oil separation at >140°C
- Maillard on cheese surface
